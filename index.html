Who We Are

Kakigōri, a Japanese shaved ice dessert, dates back to the Heian period and was first enjoyed by aristocrats. Initially mentioned in Sei Shōnagon's "The Pillow Book," it gained widespread popularity in the 19th century as ice became more accessible. The first kakigōri shop reportedly opened in Yokohama in 1869. All kakigori can be enjoyed through dine-in, takeaway or as a home pack that can be assembled and enjoyed at home. Our Kakigori are made from pasteurized dairy products and can be made vegetarian or gluten free upon request.
